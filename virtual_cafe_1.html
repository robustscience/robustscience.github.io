Wednesday, February 17, 2021
12-3pm EST, 9-noon PST
Question 1: Are your applications scalable, trustworthy, and reproducible? If yes, why and how? If not, why, and what would you need or wish to achieve the goals?
Question 2: How can publishing/dissemination/sharing standards contribute to achieving or encouraging scalability, trust, and reproducibility in results from high-throughput computation applications?
Provide us with use cases (and other examples) to support the discussion during this Virtual Café. Use cases can include applications that have (fully or partially) addressed scalability, trustworthiness, and reproducibility issues or applications that are in need of solutions for scalability, trustworthiness, and reproducibility.
